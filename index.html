April the Giraffe

April became a youtube sensation in 2017

  • Age: 16 years old
  • Location: Animal Adventure Park
  • Height: ~14 feet
  • Offspring: 4 calfs

In Febuary 2017 a livestream of April's enclosure was put up on Youtube to follow the final months of her pregnancy. It quickly gained attention, regularly having over 20k viewers watching at any given time. On April 15th, 2017 April went into labor and gave birth to a male calf, Tajiri.
